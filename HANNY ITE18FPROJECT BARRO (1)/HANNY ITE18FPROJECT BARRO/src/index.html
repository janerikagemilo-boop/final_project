‚ùì How to Play

Game Rules:

  • Two players take turns marking cells in a 3x3 grid
  • Player 1 uses X
  • Player 2 uses O
  • First player to get 3 in a row wins
  • The game can be played vertically, horizontally, or diagonally

Controls:

  • Click on any empty cell to place your mark
  • Undo Move: Take back your last move
  • New Game: Start a fresh game
  • Hint: Get a suggested move (vs AI only)

Game Modes:

  • Player vs Player: Play against a friend locally
  • Player vs Computer: Challenge the AI at different difficulty levels
