HOW TO PLAY

INSTRUCTIONS

Player 1 starts by placing an "X" in one of the empty boxes.
Player 2 then places an "O" in one of the remaining empty boxes.
Players take turns marking squares until one of the following happens:
A player wins by placing three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).
The game ends in a draw if all boxes are filled and no player has won.
After a game is over, click the Play Again button to start a new game.

TIPS & STRATEGY

Try to create "forks" - positions where you have two ways to win on your next turn
Always block your opponent if they have two in a row
The center square is the most valuable position on the board
